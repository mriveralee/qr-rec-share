Sound Share is an interface for sharing auditory information through the use of QR Codes. 

This desktop interface permits the recording and playback of sounds.

The History section provides a list of sounds created along with authorship information- a sound's title, author and date created.


The Recording section lists the sounds recorded by the user during the current session.

The interaction panel permits the recording, playback and visualization of sounds.
Recorded sounds can be shared using the upload button- the appearing upload dialog provides a means providing authorship information for a selected sound.

Upon sharing sounds are accessible to all users who have access to the QR Code.

The mobile web component permits users to play the sound history attached a QR Code but not record.
